Interesting facts about Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent birds.
  • They have outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons also have excellent hearing.
  • Pigeons are really social animals.
  • They are really strong and swift fliers because of their long wings and powerful muscles.
  • Pigeons have really good eyesight due to the fact that their eyes are on the side of their head.

Interesting facts about Pandas

  • Pandas have great camouflage.
  • They spend a lot of their day eating.
  • Bamboo makes up a huge part of a Panda's diet.
  • Panda cubs are pink, but then grow up to be black and white.

  • Pandas are loved because of their cuteness.
  • Pandas are "lazy" as eating and sleeping make up their whole day.


Origami Flowers

Click the image above to learn how to create a Origami Flower.

To make origami flowers you need a square piece of paper.

  1. Fold the paper diagonally and open it up.
  2. Fold the paper diagonally the other way and open it back up.
  3. Turn the paper over.
  4. Fold the paper in half vertically.
  5. Fold the paper in half horizantolly.
  6. Turn the paper over again.
  7. Tuck the sides of the paper in and fold it so it looks like a triangle.
  8. Fold the flap into the middle.
  9. Fold it down again to make a small triangle.
  10. Open the flap and fold it down so it forms a square.
  11. Repeat with the other flaps.
  12. Fold the triangular flap horizantolly.
  13. Flip the paper over.
  14. Fold down the part that is sticking up so it forms a triangle.
  15. Repeat with the other one.
  16. Flip the paper over again.
  17. Take one square and fold it over.
  18. Do the same with the one diagonal to it.
  19. Start twisting the center of the flower.
  20. Take a toothpick and curl the ends of the squares.
  21. That's it!
